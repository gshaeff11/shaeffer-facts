Common Types in Geology

  • Igneous Rocks: Formed from the cooling and solidification of magma or lava.
  • Sedimentary Rocks: Created by the deposition and compaction of sediments over time.
  • Metamorphic Rocks: Altered by heat, pressure, or chemically active fluids.
  • Minerals: Naturally occurring inorganic substances with a specific chemical composition and crystal structure.
